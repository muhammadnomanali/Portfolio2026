Who I Am

I am a graduate student at Auburn University pursuing a master's degree in Technical and Professional Communication. My interdisciplinary curriculum merges advanced technical writing and editing skills with a solid understanding of construction management, cost estimation, and building systems.

I study how digital communication influences trust, safety, and action. My work examines how people interpret uncertainty online and how writers and designers can reduce harm through clearer, more inclusive communication.

My passion for the built environment and sustainable construction was solidified through coursework in Construction Project Estimating and Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) systems. I am eager to connect with professionals in the architecture, engineering, and construction (AEC) industry to explore career opportunities where I can contribute my unique combination of technical communication expertise and construction knowledge.
